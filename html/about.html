About 'Welcome to School"


The aim of this site was to be an educational tool for children, inspired by flashcards in order to help them learn vocabulary in other languages.
A grid of images are displayed and they can try and remember the translation. To check if they are correct simply hover over the image and the answer will be revealed.



This site was created by Louise Davidson, Charlie Harwood and Phillipa Conlin.

This is our first ever website, built whilst attending Code First: Girls Level 1 beginners course.
We know it's the BEST website, nor the BEST LOOKING or the most TECHNICALLY CORRECT and filled with inconsistencies and mistakes but it has helped us practice what we have learnt, experiment (lot's of that one!) and hopefully the next ones will be even better.
